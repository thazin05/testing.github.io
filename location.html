Our Location

Conveniently located in Kalaw, Kalaw Heritage Hotel is a great base from which to explore this vibrant city. From here, guests can enjoy easy access to all that the lively city has to offer. For sightseeing options and local attractions, one need not look far as the hotel enjoys close proximity to Palaung Village, Shwe Oo Min Pagoda, Myin Ma Hti Cave. Offering hotel guests superior services and a broad range of amenities, Kalaw Hotel is committed to ensuring that your stay is as comfortable as possible. The hotel provides restaurant, bar, business center, concierge, laundry service to ensure our guests the greatest comfort. Experience high quality room facilities, including mini bar, complimentary bottled water, coffee/tea maker, television, shower, to help you recharge after a long day. The hotel offers many unique recreational opportunities such as garden, tennis courts. Superb facilities and an excellent location make the Kalaw Heritage Hotel the perfect base from which to enjoy your stay in Kalaw.
